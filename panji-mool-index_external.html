How to read this catalogue

The catalogue is an index of observed mūla, mūlagrāma, heading, and place candidates. High counts mean frequent appearance in the parsed corpus; they do not by themselves prove a final canonical status. Use the record search page to inspect exact source lines.

dev = Devanagari formIAST = display Roman formgotra context = nearby or table-derived associationtop sources = most frequent source files
